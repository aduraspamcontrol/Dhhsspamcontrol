About Our Grants Program

The DHHS GRANTS PROVIDER helps people from all backgrounds — whether you're working, a student, retired, young or old, deaf or hearing, widowed, or living with a disability. We provide grants and financial support to ease life's burdens.

Our mission is to ensure that financial constraints never stand in the way of health, education, or quality of life. We've helped thousands of individuals and families access the resources they need to thrive.
